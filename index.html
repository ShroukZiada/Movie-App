Frequently Asked Questions

What is MovieNight?

MovieNight is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more on thousands of internet-connected devices.

How much does MovieNight cost?

Watch MovieNight on your smartphone, tablet, Smart TV, laptop, or streaming device, all for one fixed monthly fee. Plans range from EGP120 to EGP200 a month. No extra costs, no contracts.

You can also download your favorite shows with the iOS, Android, or Windows 10 app. Use downloads to watch while you're on the go and without an internet connection. Take MovieNight with you anywhere.

Where can I watch?

Watch anywhere, anytime. Sign in with your MovieNight account to watch instantly on the web at MovieNight.com from your personal computer or on any internet-connected device that offers the MovieNight app, including smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, streaming media players and game consoles.

How do I cancel?

MovieNight is flexible. There are no pesky contracts and no commitments. You can easily cancel your account online in two clicks. There are no cancellation fees – start or stop your account anytime..

What can I watch on MovieNight?

MovieNight has an extensive library of feature films, documentaries, TV shows, anime, award-winning MovieNight originals, and more. Watch as much as you want, anytime you want.

Is MovieNight good for kids?

MovieNight The Kids experience is included in your membership to give parents control while kids enjoy family-friendly TV shows and movies in their own space.
